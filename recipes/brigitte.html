Madmoiselle Brigitte

Feel like an international sex symbol after one (or five) of these bourbon based beauties.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 oz bourbon whiskey
  • 3/4 oz brandy
  • 1/4 oz sugar syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 oz cream

How to Make:

  1. Shake over ice cubes in a shaker.
  2. Strain into your fanciest cocktail glass and enjoy!
