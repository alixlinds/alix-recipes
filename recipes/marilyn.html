Marilyn Monroe

An absolute icon - what more can be said?

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 once apple brandy
  • 1 teaspoon grenadine
  • 3/4 cup champagne or sparkling wine
  • 2 maraschino cherries

    • How to Make:

    1. Measure the apple brandy and grenadine into a cocktail shaker.
    2. Add enough ice to cover the liquid and shake for 30 seconds to ensure the brandy and grenadine are very cold.
    3. Strain into a martini glass and top up the glass with chilled champagne or sparkling wine.
    4. Skewer one or two maraschino cherries and place them into the glass. Serve immediately.
