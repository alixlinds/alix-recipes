The Gregory Peck

Made for a man of distinction, this will have the ladies' flocking.

Ingredients:

  • handful of blackberries
  • 75ml of Cinzano sweet vermouth
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 175ml of Prosecco

How to Make:

  1. Stir together blackberries, vermouth and sugar. Let stand for 1 hour until juices are released and sugar dissolves.
  2. Spoon 2 tbsp blackberry juice and 2-3 blackberries into a champagne flute or coupe.
  3. Top with Processco and enjoy!
